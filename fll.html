What is FIRST LEGO League (FLL)?

FIRST LEGO League (FLL) is an international robotics program that inspires young minds to explore STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) through hands-on learning and teamwork. Students design, build, and program LEGO robots to solve real-world challenges while developing critical skills such as problem-solving, collaboration, and creativity.

Requirements

To participate in the FIRST LEGO League (FLL) program, students must meet the following requirements:

  • Age: 9 to 14 years old
  • Interest in robotics, programming, and teamwork
  • Commitment to attend weekly team meetings and practice sessions
  • Willingness to participate in regional and national competitions
  • Parental consent and support for team activities

Program Calendar

Stay updated with our program schedule and important dates:

About AI Future

At AI Future, we inspire and empower the next generation of innovators through robotics, STEM education, and creative problem-solving. Our mission is to foster curiosity, collaboration, and a passion for technology in students of all ages.

"Our vision is to create a world where every child has the tools and confidence to shape the future through innovation and technology."

- AI Future Team

Our Core Values

Innovation

Encouraging creativity and forward-thinking to solve real-world challenges.

Collaboration

Teamwork and shared learning are at the heart of everything we do.

Curiosity

Inspiring students to ask questions and explore the unknown.

Empowerment

Providing tools and knowledge to help students shape their future.

📚 Upcoming Events

Don't miss out on our exciting upcoming events!

FTC Orientation

Dates: Week of April 28 – May 2, 2025

Join us for our FTC Orientation! This is a great opportunity for new and returning students to meet the coaches, learn about the upcoming season, and get an overview of our FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) program structure, expectations, and goals.

FTC Open Class

Date: May 3, 2025

Curious about FTC? Come experience a live demonstration! Our Open Class welcomes students, families, and anyone interested in robotics to explore what our FTC program offers — including hands-on robot driving, programming previews, and team collaboration exercises.

Grand Opening: New Location!

Date: May 2, 2025

We are excited to announce the grand opening of our brand new education center! Celebrate with us as we unveil expanded facilities, meet the team, tour the space, and enjoy a day of interactive robotics and STEM activities.

Battle Bot Event

Date: June 1, 2025

It's time to battle! Students will design, build, and compete in our exciting Battle Bot Event. This hands-on competition encourages creativity, engineering skills, and teamwork. Open to all students who want to test their robotics skills in a thrilling, friendly contest!

Summer Camp 2025

Dates: June 30 – August 22, 2025

Get ready for an exciting summer of learning, building, and fun! Our Summer Camp kicks off on June 30 and runs through August 22. Students will dive into hands-on robotics, coding, engineering design challenges, and STEM activities, all while building friendships and developing real-world skills.

First Day Reminder: Summer Camp starts soon — don’t miss out on this incredible opportunity!

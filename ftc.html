What is FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC)?

FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) is a robotics program for students aged 12-18 that challenges them to design, build, and program robots to compete in exciting head-to-head challenges. FTC fosters innovation, teamwork, and critical thinking while preparing students for careers in STEM.

Requirements

To participate in the FTC program, students must meet the following requirements:

  • Age: 12 to 18 years old
  • Interest in robotics, engineering, and programming
  • Commitment to attend weekly team meetings and practice sessions
  • Willingness to participate in regional and national competitions
  • Parental consent and support for team activities

Program Calendar

Stay updated with our program schedule and important dates:

  • September: Team registration and kickoff
  • October - December: Weekly practice sessions
  • January: Regional competitions
  • February: National competitions
  • March - April: Off-season workshops

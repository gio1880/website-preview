FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) Program

Inspiring students through advanced robotics, engineering, and teamwork.

Requirements

To participate in the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) program, students must meet the following requirements:

  • Age: 12 to 18 years old
  • Interest in robotics, engineering, and programming
  • Commitment to attend weekly team meetings and practice sessions
  • Willingness to participate in regional and national competitions
  • Parental consent and support for team activities

Skills You'll Learn

By participating in the FTC program, students will develop the following skills:

Engineering Design

Learn to design, build, and optimize robots for advanced challenges.

Programming

Gain hands-on experience with coding in Java and other programming languages.

Team Collaboration

Work with teammates to solve complex problems and achieve shared goals.

Critical Thinking

Develop innovative solutions to real-world engineering challenges.

Curriculum

The FTC program focuses on advanced robotics and engineering concepts. The curriculum includes:

  • Robot design and construction
  • Programming in Java
  • Engineering notebook documentation
  • Team collaboration and project management
  • Participation in regional and national competitions

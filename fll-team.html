Innovation Project

Archaeological Cave Mapping Rover - Safely documenting fragile historical sites while protecting both researchers and artifacts.

Problem Statement

Many caves and ancient temples contain valuable archaeological information, but their complex layouts, fragile structures, limited visibility, and safety risks make them difficult to document accurately. Traditional mapping methods can expose researchers to dangerous conditions and may unintentionally damage sensitive features. Without precise and ethical mapping techniques, important historical, cultural, and scientific data may be lost, misinterpreted, or inaccessible for future research and education.

Robot Design & Strategy

From lessons learned to innovative solutions - our journey of robot evolution and strategic development for the Masterpiece challenge.

Robot Evolution & Design Decisions

Last Year's Challenges

Our previous robot used two attachment motors‚Äîone in the front and one in the back. While this seemed flexible on paper, real practice revealed major issues:

  • Different motor heights made the back motor difficult to use
  • Front plate pulled off during attachment swaps
  • Unstable and unreliable performance

Last Year's Robot

Two-motor design with front/back configuration

Current Robot

Dual front-mounted motors with pinless attachment system

Summer Redesign Success

Over the summer, we completely redesigned based on our learnings:

  • Both motors at the front - Same height, easier access
  • Motors laying flat - More stable attachment insertion
  • Pinless system - Gravity-assisted attachment locking
  • Gear locking - Reduced wobble for heavy attachments

Innovative Pinless Attachment System

Gravity Assist

Drop-In Design

Attachments sit on top with axle dropping into holes - gravity helps hold them in place

Gear Locking

Stability System

Added gears lock attachments and reduce wobble, especially for heavier components

Quick Change

Rapid Swapping

Faster, simpler, and more reliable attachment system for quick mission transitions

Programming Improvements & Strategy

Pybricks Programming Revolution

We upgraded from SPIKE Prime block coding to Pybricks (MicroPython) for enhanced control:

  • More loop iterations - Smoother, more responsive control
  • Direct gyro access - Real-time rotation and heading data
  • Motor encoder precision - Exact distance and rotation control
  • PID corrections - Automatic drift compensation and smooth motion

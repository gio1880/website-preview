Our Curriculum

Our summer camp offers a diverse curriculum designed to engage and inspire children of all ages. Activities include arts and crafts, sports, science experiments, team-building exercises, and more. Each week has a unique theme to keep the experience fresh and exciting!

LEGO Spike Prime

Build and program robots using LEGO Spike Prime kits.

Explore advanced robotics concepts with hands-on activities!

LEGO Essential

Learn the basics of robotics with LEGO Essential kits.

Perfect for beginners to spark creativity and problem-solving skills!

FTC Robotics

Compete in exciting robotics challenges with FTC kits.

Take your robotics skills to the next level with team-based challenges!

Chess

Sharpen your mind with strategic chess games.

Learn strategies and tactics to become a chess master!

Test Prep

Prepare for exams with engaging and interactive lessons.

Boost your confidence and ace your tests with ease!

English

Improve your reading, writing, and communication skills.

Enhance your language skills through fun and interactive activities!

Exciting Trips

We organize weekly trips to local attractions such as nature parks, museums, and water parks. These trips provide a fun and educational experience for campers, ensuring they create lasting memories.

Schedule

The camp runs from June 15th to August 15th, Monday through Friday, from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM. Extended care is available from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM for an additional fee.

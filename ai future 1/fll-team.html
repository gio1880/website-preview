Innovation Project

Archaeological Cave Mapping Rover - Safely documenting fragile historical sites while protecting both researchers and artifacts.

Problem Statement

Many caves and ancient temples contain valuable archaeological information, but their complex layouts, fragile structures, limited visibility, and safety risks make them difficult to document accurately. Traditional mapping methods can expose researchers to dangerous conditions and may unintentionally damage sensitive features. Without precise and ethical mapping techniques, important historical, cultural, and scientific data may be lost, misinterpreted, or inaccessible for future research and education.

4) Programming

Pybricks (MicroPython) Upgrade

We transitioned from SPIKE Prime block coding to Pybricks for finer control and reliability.

  • Higher loop frequency for smoother motion
  • Direct gyro access for accurate heading control
  • Encoder precision for exact distance and rotations
  • PID corrections for drift compensation

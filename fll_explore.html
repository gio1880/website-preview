FIRST LEGO League Explore Program

Inspiring young minds through creativity, exploration, and teamwork.

What is FIRST LEGO League Explore?

FIRST LEGO League Explore is a hands-on STEM program designed for younger students, typically aged 6 to 10 years old. It introduces children to the exciting world of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics through playful exploration and teamwork. Students work together to design and build LEGO models and create a Show Me poster to present their ideas.

Requirements

To participate in the FIRST LEGO League Explore program, students must meet the following requirements:

  • Age: 6 to 10 years old
  • Interest in creativity, building, and teamwork
  • Commitment to attend weekly team meetings and activities
  • Willingness to present their projects at local showcases
  • Parental consent and support for team activities

Program Calendar

Stay updated with our program schedule and important dates:

  • January: Team registration and orientation
  • February - April: Weekly exploration sessions
  • May: Local showcase event
  • June: End-of-season celebration
  • July - August: Optional summer workshops
